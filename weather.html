Welcome to my homemade WMAD Weather Network.

Using the OpenWeather API, I have produced the local weather reports for the day in Moncton. Sometimes I forget to check the weather before leaving my house and regret it later. I figured with a centralized website that has our schedules AND the weather it might help me be better prepared with winter coming.
