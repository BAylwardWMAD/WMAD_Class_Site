Latest Updates - Aug 21st, 2021

  • We are currently on track to return on the 8th of September, 2021
  • Health Concerns: We will be returning to campus for in-person instruction 2-3 days per week. All instructors have been mandated to get the COVID-19 Vaccine, otherwise they will be frequently tested in accordance with public health guidelines.
  • The student body currently doesn't require the vaccine, however it is strongly encouraged. (Obvious medical concerns are exceptions), in which case I believe we should maintain as much social distancing as possible.

    Even if we are not required to social distance, personally I will maintain a healthy distance to protect my family from any potential exposures.
